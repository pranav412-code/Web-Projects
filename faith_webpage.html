THE FAITH FORUM

About us

FAITH, which stands for Forum for All In technical Harmony is a forum to nurture the talent of students and to furnish them. There are 1080 students in first year of GHRCE. To provide these students a atomsphere to grow and to accomplish milestones in college, We the Faith forum comprises 6 different committees i.e. Technical, Cultural, Sports, Discipline, Anchoring and Magazine which are supervised by 3 Vice Presidents. To take care of all the stuff going on in forum we have Back End Secretary and lastly the heart of this is SIR! President.

Events

Under the guidance of Faith Incharge-GHRCE, our forum Faith conduct events, manage the entire curriculum of first year and enhance the quality experience of first year department.

Photography and reel making competition

The first event, faith forum conducted was Photography and reel making competition under Astronomy Club from 1st to 4th May. Students were told to click a picture abstract with a quaote. To make event more fruitful, variety of content such as professional reels, vlogging experience from students had been collected. Winners were felicited with exciting gift hampers.

The Quiz Contest

From 27th May-28th May Faith Forum Conducted Quiz Contest. The event had been conducted in 2 rounds, 1st round was conducted on 27th and 2nd round on 28th May. Only Qualified participants of 1st round were eligible for 2nd round. About 6 teams were qualified for 2nd round and a the end 2 teams were win the quiz who were felicited from Dean Madam FY and Faith Incharge.

Mind Debugger

Another stepping stone towards enhancing the youth of today and the future of tomorrow. A four day workshop " Mind Debugger " has been conducted from 6th July to 9th of July at G.H. Raisoni College of Engineering, Nagpur. This workshop was scheduled to train young minds and give them a better view of the numerous career options regardless of programming.It was organised by the organizing team of first year forum FAITH.

The seminar was delivered by Professor Pranay Saraf, Incharge Alumni Cell, GHRCE.The areas of focus covered by Pranay Sir were Full Stack Development, essentials of Programming, Deep knowledge of coding Languages.It has been an interactive session where students reciprocated with the faculty where none of their questions were left unanswered.Under the guidance of Dr Sachin Untawale, Director, GHRCE this session took place successfully.Dr. Bhavana Butey, Dean First Year also attended the training session as an active listener.Technical Secretary Mst Adish Bahatkar took charge of the event as a team manager with the effective leadership of Forum President Mst Aman Brijbhushan Tiwari.

The Raisoni Darbar

1st Year Faith Forum has organized an event ‘Raisoni’s Darbar’ on 16th July 2022 , under the guidance of Ms. Sindhu Panikar. The event was unique in itself because it was held for the first time in this year.

The event was divided in three rounds. Rules and regulations of the event were explained at the beginning .The first round of the competition was ‘Speech Maker’. In this round, the participants were free to choose their topic, to build up their confidence language was not a barrier in this round. English was compulsory with either Hindi or Marathi. Each participant had one minute to showcase their skills. Out of 60+ students 12 students qualified this round wisely.

The second rounds was ‘Tug of Speech War’. For this round, four teams of three students were made. Two team had a single topic. Where one was a ‘for’ and the other was ‘against’ team. Each team member had one minute to speak, and they were examined individually, out of these six students were selected for the final round.

The 3rd Round (final round) was one of the most awaited round for our participants, and that was ‘Raisoni’s Got Speaker’. Each one of the six participants had to elaborate their thoughts on either an image or a video individually. English was mandatory for this round and the time limit was 2 minutes per participant.

While the evaluation was going on, in order to maintain the spirit of the audience we had an audience round.

A moment later, we got our winner: Parth Kanojiya from CSE Cybersecurity department.

Runner up: Karan Pandey from CSE Cybersecurity department The President of First Year Faith Forum Aman Tiwari gave vote of thanks to our respected judges, teachers, audience, participants and other helping hands. The event was concluded by giving respect to our nation, by reciting the National Anthem.

A special mention to our respected Communication Skill Trainer, Ms. Sindhu Panikar for being the catalyst that stimulated us to do our best and standing as pillars of strength .With a deep sense of appreciation we thank our respected teacher, Ms. Sindhu Panikar , Ms. Pallavi Deshpande , Ms. Smita Dhote .
